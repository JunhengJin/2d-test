Water-saving tips: 
1. Wash the car with a bucket of water; 
use washing water and laundry water to wash the car; use a water-saving spray gun to wash. 
2. Shower with water intermittently, turn off the water in time when scrubbing, and avoid showering for too long. 
The water after the bath can be used for washing cars, flushing toilets, mopping floors, etc.
